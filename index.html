About Me

My introduction

My name is Razon (Bangla: মোহাম্মদ রাজন) and I am a licensed mortgage loan originator serving the State of Michigan. My family and I immigrated to the United States in Winter of 1996 and have always called Metro Detroit home since. Like many Bangladeshi immigrants, I grew up in Hamtramck, MI, attended Holbrook Elementary School, Frontier International Academy and briefly studied pre-nursing at Wayne State University. Shortly after moving to Warren, I had transferred to Oakland University and worked as a Nursing Assistant at Beaumont Hospital in Royal Oak. It was there that I learned that life was very cyclical and that you should chase your passions and find contentment in everything you do. Fast foward to leaving health care and entering the corporate side of things. I started off at United Wholesale Mortgage (UWM) in 2019 as a lending support specialist in processing. I was doing documentation for Homeowners Insurance, title packages, condo project documentation and getting into the weeds of the mortgage world. Shortly after, I became an Underwriter, which to this day, is benefitting clients because it allows me to find solutions for you before a lender even inquires about an obstacle. My last 3 years at UWM, I had lead a group of fine Senior and Junior Underwriters who I coached, reviewed loan escalations with, trained and promoted. I had left UWM in December of 2023 after completing my education, training and licensing with the NMLS and joined my very first brokerage, Planted Local Lending. It only made sense to leave the corporate side of things and join consumers in helping them succeed in obtaining a mortgage through my experience and knowledge.
